Yes, you can choose your preferred dentist at Clove Dental. We have a team of specialized dentists for different treatments.

Most of our clinics operate from 9 AM to 9 PM, seven days a week. However, timings may vary by location.

Yes, we ensure continuity of care by assigning the same doctor for your treatment unless you request otherwise.
How can I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment through our website, mobile app, by calling our customer care number, or by visiting any clinic.
How you are able to provide low prices than other dental brands?
Our large network and economies of scale allow us to negotiate better prices while maintaining high quality standards.
How frequently should I visit a dentist?
We recommend a dental check-up every six months for preventive care and early detection of any issues.
